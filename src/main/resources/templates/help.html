Help

Events

To book an event for yourself, click and drag on your calendar on the homepage and highlight the slot you wish to book.

To book an event with a staff member. Search the name of the staff member in the top right-hand corner. Don't worry if you can't spell your lecturers name as only a few letters may return them to you. Likewise, if you have no idea of even the first few letters, you can also search with a blank box to return all lecturers!

Once you have found your chosen staff member click the 'Book' button next to their name.

You will then be taken to that staff members calendar where you can proceed to book them. Remember however, you can only book staff members to half an hour slots, at least 24 hours beforehand, therefore only click and don't try and drag!

Notifications

To read your notifications click on the 'View notifications' button on the menu at the top. Please note if you have notifications, it will 'x unread notifications' instead, with x being the number of unread notifications.

On the 'Your notifications' page, the unread notifications will have a star with a coloured background next to them.

To read all notifications, click the 'Read all notifications' button at the top.

To read just one notification, click the 'view' button next to the notifications.

To click through to the entity that notified you, click the 'view' button, under the 'Link' column when viewing one notification.

Account Details

To view your account details, click the 'Account Details' on the menu at the top of the page.

Here you can view the details of your account as well as edit, or delete, your account.

To edit something on your account, click the 'edit' button next to what you would like to edit. Please note, editing your email or password will log you out.

To delete your account, click the delete account button.
